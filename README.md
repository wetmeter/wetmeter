# WetMeter — River Route Map Navigation

🗺️ **Plan your water routes on an interactive map.**

WetMeter is a free web tool for paddlers — kayak, canoe, and SUP enthusiasts — to create and manage river routes directly on a map.

## 🚀 **Features:**

*   **Start plotting your route:** Simply click the "Start" button and begin tracing your path along the river.
*   **Map Layer Options:** Choose your preferred map layer (e.g., OpenStreetMap, satellite imagery, CARTO) for optimal navigation.
*   **Route Creation:** Draw water routes directly on OpenStreetMap.
*   **Waypoints:** Mark important points of interest (campsites, parking, beaches, put-ins/take-outs).
*   **Export & Share:**
    *   **Share your route:** Save and share your routes with friends by sending a unique link.
    *   **Export files:** Export routes in **KML** (for Google Earth) and **GPX** (for most GPS devices) formats.
*   **Cross-Platform:** Works on both mobile and desktop devices.
*   **Multilingual:** Intuitive interface available in 8 languages.

---

## ✨ **How it works:**

1.  Click the **"Start"** button to enter route plotting mode.
2.  Select your preferred **map layer** for easy navigation.
3.  Click on the map to place points along your route.
4.  Add markers for important spots (campsites, parking, etc.).
5.  **Save & Share:**
    *   Click the export button to get a link to your route.
    *   Choose **KML** or **GPX** to export the route file.

## 🛠️ **Tech Stack:**

*   Vanilla JavaScript (no frameworks)
*   Leaflet + OpenStreetMap
*   BRouter for route calculation
*   Static hosting on VPS
